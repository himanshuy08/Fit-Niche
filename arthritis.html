Exercise for Arthritis

The following of exercise may help relief the pain ,
joint stiffness and other symptoms that rheumatoid arthritis(RA) can cause :

  1. Stretching
  2. Walking
  3. Flowing movements , such as tai chi and yoga
  4. Pilates
  5. Water Exercise
  6. Cycling
  7. Strenght Training
  8. Hand Exercise
  9. Gardening
